The Jubilee Mission Group of Institutions comprises specialized colleges offering focused, industry-aligned programs that combine cutting-edge theory with extensive clinical training, preparing professionals for rewarding careers globally.

Nursing Programs

**The Backbone of Patient Care.** These programs are offered through the **Jubilee Mission College of Nursing**. We focus on developing compassionate, skilled, and evidence-based practitioners, providing intensive training in clinical procedures, critical care, and professional ethics.

Programs typically offered:

  • B.Sc. Nursing (4 Years)
  • Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing (2 Years)
  • GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery)

BPT (Physiotherapy)

**Restoring Movement and Quality of Life.** This program is conducted by the **Jubilee Mission Institute of Physiotherapy**. Students delve into movement science and therapeutic techniques, gaining expert knowledge in musculoskeletal, neurological, and cardio-respiratory rehabilitation.

Program Details:

  • Bachelor of Physiotherapy (4.5 Years including internship)
  • Focus on Orthopedic, Neuro, and Sports rehab
  • Extensive clinical practice required

B.Sc. Allied Health Sciences

**Diagnostics and Technical Support.** These specialized programs are run by the **Jubilee Mission Institute of Allied Health Sciences**. They train students for technical roles crucial to modern diagnostics and patient management, emphasizing precision and technical competence.

Specializations (B.Sc. 3 Years):

  • B.Sc. Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT)
  • B.Sc. Medical Imaging Technology (MIT)
  • B.Sc. Anaesthesia Technology / Operation Theatre (AT/OT)
