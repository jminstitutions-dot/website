Our World-Class Campus

A Hub of Learning and Innovation

The Jubilee Mission Group of Institutions is strategically **located in the heart of the city**, ensuring unparalleled accessibility and connectivity for our students. This prime location offers the dual advantage of a serene academic environment combined with the cultural and professional opportunities of a major metropolitan hub.

We boast **modern infrastructure** designed to facilitate advanced healthcare education. This includes spacious, technology-enabled lecture halls, dedicated simulation rooms, and high-speed campus-wide Wi-Fi. Our commitment to student convenience is reflected in the **excellent transport** links surrounding the campus, making daily commuting simple for both local and commuting students.

The campus environment is deliberately holistic, offering comfortable residential facilities, recreational areas, and secure spaces, all contributing to a balanced and enriching student life.

The Knowledge Hub: Central Library

Our central **Library** is the nerve center of academic activity, offering students and faculty access to a vast collection of resources crucial for **Nursing, Physiotherapy, and Allied Health Sciences**. We house an extensive range of textbooks, national and international journals, reference materials, and a rapidly expanding collection of **e-resources**.

The library features tranquil, air-conditioned reading rooms, dedicated study carrels, and a digital section equipped with high-speed internet and access to online databases. We believe in fostering a culture of lifelong learning and research, making the library an indispensable resource for every student's academic journey.

Sports & Wellness Facilities

We recognize that physical wellness is paramount, especially for future healthcare professionals. Our **Sports** facilities are designed to encourage active student participation in various indoor and outdoor games.

We provide dedicated spaces for **badminton, basketball, indoor games** like chess and carrom, and a well-equipped **gymnasium**. These activities promote teamwork, discipline, and stress reduction, contributing significantly to the overall well-being and holistic development of our student body. Regular inter-college events and fitness programs are organized throughout the year.

Advanced Clinical & Anatomy Laboratories

Practical expertise is non-negotiable in healthcare. Our **Laboratories** are equipped with cutting-edge technology and simulation models to provide realistic, hands-on training.

Key facilities include the dedicated **Anatomy and Physiology Labs**, a fully functional **Nursing Foundation Lab** with sophisticated manikins, and specialized **Physiotherapy Labs** for electrotherapy and exercise therapy. These labs ensure students can bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and clinical application in a controlled, supervised environment.

All equipment is maintained to the highest standards, mirroring the setups found in modern hospital environments, preparing students for real patient care right from day one.
